state house of assembly standing committees on higher education and health have visited yusuf maitama sule university,kano(YUMSUK). speaking during the visit,the chairman of higher education Committee,Hon.Nuhu Abdullahi Achika Said they were at the University for a meeting with its management for input in respect of the Muhammad Abdullahi Wase Teaching Hospital Bill. The Bill is for conversion of the Hopital to YUMSUK teaching Hospital.They commended the Vice-chancellor for the remarkable developments recorded in the University. " Yusuf Maitama Sule University,Kano is taking proactive measures towards ensuring that it produces qualitative medical doctors for the state and beyond ,"he told the visitors. Prof kurawa , who received members of the committees in his office at the main campus of the University,renewed committment towards uplifting the standard of the institution.

"Prof. Kurawa said:The University is making effort to ensure sound footing of its medicine pprogramme, through which it intended to produce qualified medical doctors in the society."
